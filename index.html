FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

Do you clean the window frames and sills?
Yes! We don't just clean the glass. Every standard window clean includes the frames and sills at no extra cost. It's part of our "whole window" philosophy.
Do I need to be home when you clean?
Not at all. As long as we have access to the rear of your property (side gate etc.), we can carry out the work. We can leave a card or send a text to let you know we've been.
How often should I get my windows cleaned?
Most of our residential customers in Clacton prefer a 4-week or 8-week cycle. This keeps your windows looking great year-round. We can discuss a schedule that suits your budget.
How do I pay?
We try to make it easy. We accept Cash, Bank Transfer (BACS), or Cheque. Most customers prefer to transfer the money online after receiving our invoice text.
